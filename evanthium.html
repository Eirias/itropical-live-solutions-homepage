Evanthium

A location-based fantasy battle game. Choose your element, collect tokens in AR, conquer temples and challenge other players.

Features

Four Elements

Choose your fraction — Fire, Air, Water or Earth. Collect elemental tokens and merge all four to create rare Evanthium.

AR Coin Collector

Explore your surroundings in augmented reality. Find and collect virtual coins placed at real-world GPS locations.

PvP Battles

Challenge other players on the leaderboard. Stake tokens to attack — win and steal Evanthium from your opponents.

Temples & Map

Discover temples on the map, check in for protection and tribute tokens for rewards. Location-based gameplay meets strategy.

Quests & Rewards

Complete daily quests including movement challenges tracked via HealthKit. Earn elemental tokens and level up.

Leaderboard

Compete globally or filter by element. Track battles fought, coins collected and player levels.
